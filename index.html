About Me

Hi, I'm Chris, a games programmer currently based in Lincoln, UK. I've been making games for 7 years and I now develop VR games professionally. I graduated from University of Lincoln , with a Upper-Second class Honours in BSc in Games Computing in 2021.

My main interests are gameplay, Virtual Reality, and multiplayer. I love creating gameplay mechanics for games and custom systems. My main passion is VR development as it's such a niche area in the games industry at the moment. Being able to make unique gameplay in VR and making it an enjoyable player experience is what makes VR development so fun for me. I also enjoy making multiplayer games as well, as it puts a new challenge into making games.
Feel free to look through all of my projects below, I am also looking for freelance work, so if you're interested in my projects feel free to contact me.
